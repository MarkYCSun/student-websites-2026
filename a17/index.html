How to play

  • ← → move · ↑ rotate · ↓ soft drop · Space hard drop
  • Fill a whole row to clear it. Stack carefully—if new pieces cannot spawn, game over.

Scoring & levels

  • Line clears only: 1 line = 100, 2 = 300, 3 = 500, 4 = 800 (then ×2 during Chaos).
  • When your total score reaches 500, you go to Level 2; every next 500 points moves you up again (1000 → Level 3, and so on).
  • Each level drops blocks a bit faster.
  • Passing 200, 400, 600… starts Chaos: 60s of ×2 line score (timer top-right). Center shows BONUS TIME briefly (nothing resets your total).
