HOW TO SURVIVE

Controls

  • ←/→ drift left / right
  • ↓ soft drop (descend)
  • ↑ rotate clockwise
  • Space hard drop (damn it)
  • Z rotate counter-clockwise
  • C / Shift hold (entomb)
  • P / Esc pause

The Horrors

  • Dracula — 4 attacks. Clear 3 lines to defeat.
  • Werewolf — slashes the board at level 7+.
  • Ghost — inverts controls if touched.
  • Spider — webs blocks, spreads over time.
  • Grim Reaper — rises as your stack rises.

Tools of Survival

  • Garlic — repels the next monster event.
  • Silver Cross — clears cursed / webbed blocks.
  • Witch Brew — random fate, good or ill.
  • Death Rattle — clears 5 rows, costs 1000.
  • Cursed Candle — rising flames clear rows.
  • Bat Familiar — devours blocks for 30s.
